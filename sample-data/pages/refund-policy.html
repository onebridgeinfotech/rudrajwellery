Cancellation & Refund Policy

Last updated: June 2026

At Rudra Jewellery we want you to love your purchase. Please read this policy before ordering.

Imitation jewellery

All products are fashion/imitation jewellery unless stated otherwise. Slight variation in colour or finish may occur and is not considered a defect.

Earrings — hygiene policy

Due to hygiene and safety, earrings cannot be returned or exchanged unless you received the wrong item or a damaged piece.

Eligible returns

  • Wrong item shipped
  • Damaged or defective on arrival — report within 48 hours of delivery with clear photos
  • Missing items from your order

Non-refundable

  • Change of mind or size/colour preference
  • Minor shade differences in stones or plating
  • Orders cancelled after UPI payment was verified and packing has started
  • Items used, altered, or without original packaging

Cancellations

Orders may be cancelled before payment verification. After UPI is confirmed, cancellation depends on whether packing has begun — contact us immediately on WhatsApp.

UPI refunds

Approved refunds are processed to the original UPI account within 7–10 business days after we receive and inspect returned items (where applicable).

How to request

Email your@email.com or WhatsApp +91 7730817950 with your order number, photos, and reason within 48 hours of delivery.
