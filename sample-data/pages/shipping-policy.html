Shipping & Delivery Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Processing time

Orders are processed after your UPI payment and UTR are verified (usually within 24 hours on working days). You will receive an email when your order is confirmed and when it ships.

Delivery areas

We ship across India via trusted courier partners. Remote or pin-code restricted areas may take longer.

Estimated delivery

  • 5–10 business days after dispatch, depending on your location
  • Festival seasons and weather may cause occasional delays
  • Free shipping on orders above ₹999 (when offer is active)

Shipping charges

Applicable charges are shown at checkout before you pay. We pack items securely to prevent damage in transit.

Tracking

Tracking details are sent by email when available. You can also use our Track Order page with your order ID and billing email.

Undelivered / RTO

If a parcel is returned due to an incorrect address or repeated failed delivery attempts, re-shipping charges may apply. Please ensure your shipping details are accurate.
